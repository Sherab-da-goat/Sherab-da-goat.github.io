CNRG

Originating from the sunny green fields of Tallaght Dublin, CNRG's journey through the electronic music is a defiant departure from the ordinary. Forming his sonic identity through years spent in Ibiza and exploring all that European clubs had to offer he found solace within Amsterdam. Where he further sculpted amidst the ecstatic pulse of the cities energy.

Now a cornerstone of Code Red, CNRG’s artistry rebels against the typical. His productions are skillful act of balance—melding the raw energy of warehouse techno with the transcendent energy of trance. His sound is characterized by bold, driving kicks and a penchant for atmospheric depth, setting stages alight with an unmistakable groove tailored for the apex of the night.

His technique? A homage to the chaos of creativity, influenced by giants like Tiësto and Ben Klock, yet distinctly his own. CNRG's command of the mixer is an exhilarating dance of swift transitions, embodying the art of live performance. The year 2024 marks a pivotal era for CNRG as he channels his distinctive interpretation of techno to a global audience. Memorable performances at Club Eden in Ibiza and a celebrated introduction at Dublin's Index have solidified his reputation. With Code Red's backing, CNRG's impact on the electronic music scene is undeniable. We're excited to have CNRG in the Code Red family, a genuine talent driving the collective’s spirit forward and setting the stage for the future of electronic music
